Joyce's Biography

Being a Taiwanese student in the States, she seeks to maximize her cultural and language background at school and at work. As a current senior, Joyce is looking to expand her knowledge by taking classes related to web development, digital strategy and analytics, while working as a full-time intern at Warner Chappell Music.
